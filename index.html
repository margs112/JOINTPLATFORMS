ALAB LASALYANO +

From heartfelt songs to powerful plays, witness the talent of Lasallians from Grade School to Senior High School, all coming together for a cause that matters. Awit at Alab Lasalyano is a concert-for-a-cause that showcases not just singing and dancing, but also theatrical performances, spoken word, live art, and more. As the stage comes alive, so does our mission. A live donation tracker will keep everyone updated in real time, ensuring transparency as we raise funds to support those in need.

#SHSWRAPPED +

You want access to your Lasallian memories — we got you! What better way to have it than yearbooks! Through this endeavor, classes will be called to submit their section yearbooks at the end of the academic year for submission in a CSYA-part contest. This will not only contribute to class spirit, but also, foster a warm environment for every class that is united in keeping record of their best memories and year-defining highlights.
